Organization Status &
Functioning

Aniketana Trust is registered under Indian Trust Act 1882. This has 12A, 80G and registration with NGO Darpan under NITI Aayog, Government of India. This is registered for the Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) programs with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), Government of India.

In order to have the overall management and functioning, the Trust comprises of Board of Trustees, Directors, General Manager, Program Manager, the Program Coordinators, Finance & Accounts Executive. They are helped by a group of facilitators based across the state. They set goals, formulate plans and budgets, integrate the operations and functions, create synergy, evolve policies and guidelines, review and monitor the organization’s performance, interact and nurture relationships with internal as well as external stakeholders.
