Rural Youth Through Education

Aniketana Trust is a not-for-profit and non-political public charitable Trust which is registered under the Indian Trust Act 1882 in 2012. The Trust is based in Yadgir, Karnataka of India and works for the people in the state of Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh without having any bias of race, caste, creed, religion and community. The main focus of the Trust is to support the needy and meritorious youth in their educational pursuit through the provision of educational scholarship, mentoring and career guidance. In this process, a youth is supported with a holistic manner for his or her education, moral, social and professional life. Over the three decades, the Trust has impacted the lives of more than 5000 youth, their families and the communities bringing significant changes in the situations through its impact driven educational intervention. This holistic development work is being carried out through collaborations with institutes, individual donors, donor agencies and other partners. Apart from this, the Trust also works for the schools, young students and undertakes other programs. Over the decades the trust has impacted more than 5000 students, helping them build educational and professional futures.
